Our Process

Place Order

Begin your journey by placing an order through our online form or by contacting us directly. Provide us with basic details about your business and your website requirements

Initial Consultation

Once your order is received, We schedule an initial consultation to discuss your goals, preferences, and specific needs. This helps us understand your vision and tailor our approach accordingly.

Project Proposal and Quote

After the consultation, We create a detailed project proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and cost of the project. We provide a transparent quote and await your approval to proce.

70 % Payment

Upon approval of the project proposal, We require a 70% upfront payment to commence the work. This payment ensures the commitment and resources needed for your project..

Design Phase

Our design team starts by creating wireframes and mockups based on your input. We focus on creating a user-friendly layout and design that aligns with your brand identity. You'll have the opportunity to review and provide feedback.

Development Phase

Once the design is approved from customer end, our developers start building your website. We use the latest technologies to ensure your site is fast, secure, and scalable. You’ll be updated regularly on the progress.

Content Integration

We work with you to gather and integrate all necessary content, including text, images, videos, and other media. If needed, Our team can talk you with content creation to ensure everything aligns with your brand voice.

Testing and Quality Assurance

Before the launch, we conduct thorough testing to identify and fix any issues. This includes cross-browser testing, mobile responsiveness checks, and performance optimization to ensure a flawless user experience.

Review

We present the fully developed website for your review. Any final changes or adjustments are made based on your feedback to ensure the site meets your expectations.

Revisions and Modifications.

We present the fully developed website for your review. Any necessary revisions and modifications are made based on your feedback to ensure the site meets your expectations.

Rest 30 % Payment

Before Pre-launch of your website Successfully, the remaining 30% payment is due. This final payment completes the project transaction.

Finally Launch

Upon your approval, We finalize the website and prepare it for launch. We handle all technical aspects, including domain setup.Server deployment,Google Search Console Setup to ensure a smooth go-live process.
